
# Automated SLR Generator for an AI-Powered Electrical Engineering Research Assistant

This project presents the design and methodology of an Automated Systematic Literature Review (SLR) Generator developed as a core component of an intelligent research assistant for Electrical Engineering.

The framework integrates evidence-governed workflows, PRISMA-compliant review processes, human-in-the-loop validation, and hallucination mitigation mechanisms to support transparent, reproducible, and scientifically reliable literature synthesis.

The project focuses on transforming large collections of research papers into structured reviews, taxonomies, research trends, evidence matrices, and publication-ready SLR reports while maintaining traceability between every claim and its supporting evidence.
