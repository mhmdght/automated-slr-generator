# Automated SLR Generator for an AI-Powered Electrical Engineering Research Assistant

An evidence-governed Automated Systematic Literature Review (SLR) Generator designed as a core component of an AI-powered research assistant for Electrical Engineering.

This framework combines PRISMA-compliant workflows, evidence traceability, human-in-the-loop validation, and hallucination mitigation mechanisms to support transparent, reproducible, and scientifically reliable literature synthesis.

The proposed methodology transforms large collections of scientific publications into structured systematic reviews, research taxonomies, evidence matrices, trend analyses, and publication-ready SLR reports while maintaining full traceability between generated findings and their supporting evidence.

## Key Features

* PRISMA 2020-compliant review workflow
* Evidence-first knowledge synthesis
* Human-in-the-loop validation checkpoints
* Hallucination mitigation and uncertainty disclosure
* Research gap identification and trend analysis
* Automated taxonomy construction
* Evidence traceability and provenance tracking
* Scalable architecture for large-scale literature reviews
